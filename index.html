Power Level Game

You will be given a random Power Level at the start of the game. There are 4 brave warriors below. By clicking on a warrior your will add a specific Power Level that is needed to defeat Firelord Ozai.

You win by matching your Power Level to the random power level given at the start of the game. If you go over the Power Level given, you become too strong and kill the FireLord on accident and lose the game.

The Power Level of each warrior is hidden until you click on it

The value of Power Levels change at the start of each game.
